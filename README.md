# GlassBox: The World's First AML Co-Pilot 

**🏆 Grand Champions: Accenture University Innovation Challenge** 

## 📌 Overview
GlassBox is a human-centric Anti-Money Laundering (AML) platform designed for Financial Institutions. It addresses the core challenge: *How might we enable Due Diligence Analysts to confidently utilise and oversee AI-driven detection through adaptive workflows, supportive roles and safeguards to guide human-centric development?* 

**[🚀 View Live Prototype Here](https://jungyongjy.github.io/accenture-uic/)**

## ⚠️ The Problem
The financial industry relies on analysts to enforce AML controls amidst regulatory penalties, but current measures are limited by:
* **The "Black Box" of AI:** An absence of explainability behind conclusions made by AI/ML models when determining fraud.
* **Manual Monitoring Systems:** Over-reliance on deterministic "If-Then" rules and boolean thresholds.
* **Analyst Burnout & Liability:** Junior analysts drown in low-value repetition and manual verifications, while department heads distrust AI outputs due to personal liability.

## ✨ The Solution
Our solution features a transparent, intelligent AML Co-Pilot that does the heavy lifting while empowering human analysts to exercise judgement and decision-making. 

### Key Features
* **Dynamic Risk Profiles:** Generates risk score predictions with confidence intervals.
* **Explainable AI (XAI) & Reasoning:** Provides readable analysis so users understand exactly why an account was flagged.
* **Agentic AI Workflows:** Drafts an initial Suspicious Transaction Report (STR) in seconds, enhancing efficiency and timeliness.
* **The Golden Circle Feedback Loop:** Integrates Analyst Overrides, AI Specialist Reviews, Model Tuning, and Model Optimisation so the system becomes smarter with every interaction, continuously reducing false positives and increasing analyst trust.

## 📈 Value Proposition
* **4x** Increased suspicious activity detection.
* **60%** Reduces false positives.
* **87%** Reduces manual monitoring.

## 🚀 Scalability
The GlassBox methodology scales across horizontal industries:
* **Financial Industry:** Insurance Fraud (Assigns risk scores to flag potential fraud cases for further investigation).
* **Non-Financial Industry:** E-Commerce & Marketplace Fraud (Detect manipulation of seller listings & reviews).
