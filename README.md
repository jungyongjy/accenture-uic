## 📌 Overview
[cite_start]GlassBox is a human-centric Anti-Money Laundering (AML) platform designed for Financial Institutions[cite: 7]. [cite_start]It addresses the core challenge: *How might we enable Due Diligence Analysts to confidently utilise and oversee AI-driven detection through adaptive workflows, supportive roles and safeguards to guide human-centric development?* [cite: 8]

## ⚠️ The Problem
[cite_start]The financial industry relies on analysts to enforce AML controls amidst regulatory penalties[cite: 11], but current measures are limited by:
* [cite_start]**The "Black Box" of AI:** An absence of explainability behind conclusions made by AI/ML models when determining fraud[cite: 19].
* [cite_start]**Manual Monitoring Systems:** Over-reliance on deterministic "If-Then" rules and boolean thresholds[cite: 20, 21, 22].
* [cite_start]**Analyst Burnout & Liability:** Junior analysts drown in low-value repetition and manual verifications [cite: 38, 46, 47, 48][cite_start], while department heads distrust AI outputs due to personal liability[cite: 57, 58, 72].

## ✨ The Solution
[cite_start]Our solution features a transparent, intelligent AML Co-Pilot that does the heavy lifting while empowering human analysts to exercise judgement and decision-making[cite: 144]. 

### Key Features
* [cite_start]**Dynamic Risk Profiles:** Generates risk score predictions with confidence intervals[cite: 141].
* [cite_start]**Explainable AI (XAI) & Reasoning:** Provides readable analysis so users understand exactly why an account was flagged[cite: 109, 138, 150].
* [cite_start]**Agentic AI Workflows:** Drafts an initial Suspicious Transaction Report (STR) in seconds, enhancing efficiency and timeliness[cite: 130, 152].
* [cite_start]**The Golden Circle Feedback Loop:** Integrates Analyst Overrides, AI Specialist Reviews, Model Tuning, and Model Optimisation so the system becomes smarter with every interaction, continuously reducing false positives and increasing analyst trust[cite: 156, 157, 158, 159, 162, 163].
